Case Study: Reducing Inbox Anxiety with Agentic AI

Problem: Constant email interruptions fragment deep work and create cognitive overload. Most incoming emails don't require immediate attention, yet the inbox demands it.

Approach: An agentic AI email gatekeeper intercepts incoming Gmail messages and uses a local AI model to classify intent, compute a sentiment score, and measure emotional impact before and after toning down content. Only emails deemed actionable or urgent trigger a focused Telegram notification; the rest are quietly stored for later review.

Goal: Protect deep work, minimize interruptions, and create a calmer, more controlled communication experience without missing what actually matters.
